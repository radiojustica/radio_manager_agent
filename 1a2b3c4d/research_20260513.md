# Research: Intelligent Playlisting with Weather-Mood Mapping

**Date**: 2026-05-13

## 1. Executive Summary
The current playlist generation system uses a modular approach where `PlaylistWorker` delegates to `PlaylistEngine`, which in turn uses `GradeRules` for business logic. The research identified that while the "GestorFila" (Queue Manager) and mood-to-genre mapping exist, they are static and based on a pseudo-weather service that uses time-of-day instead of actual environmental data.

## 2. Technical Context
- **PlaylistWorker** (`workers/playlist_worker.py:14`): Acts as a thin wrapper around `PlaylistEngine`.
- **PlaylistEngine** (`director/playlist_engine.py:101`): Detects mood via `weather_service` if not provided and calls `grade_rules.montar_bloco`.
- **WeatherService** (`services/weather_service.py:6`): Currently a hardcoded heuristic based on time/day, returning 'Ensolarado', 'Nublado', or 'Chuvoso'.
- **GradeRules** (`director/grade_rules.py:73`): Contains the `MOODS` dictionary mapping moods to styles and the `GestorFila` class.
- **GestorFila** (`director/grade_rules.py:118`): Implements anti-repetition (artist/song) and priority-based shuffling.

## 3. Findings & Analysis
- **Mood Mapping**: Located in `director/grade_rules.py:73`. It maps 'Ensolarado', 'Chuvoso', and 'Nublado' to specific music styles.
- **Dayparting**: `obter_regras_energia_por_hora` (`director/grade_rules.py:59`) defines energy targets based on the hour of the day.
- **Queue Logic**: `montar_bloco` (`director/grade_rules.py:192`) implements a dynamic energy curve within each 2-hour block (Smooth -> Peak -> Smooth).
- **Weather Service Limitation**: The service (`services/weather_service.py`) is purely deterministic based on system time and does not support the "mood-based queries" requested in the ticket.

## 4. Technical Constraints
- **Database Schema**: Relies on `Musica.estilo`, `Musica.energia`, `Musica.vezes_tocada`, and `Musica.ultima_reproducao`.
- **Encoding**: M3U files must be encoded in `cp1252` for ZaraRadio compatibility (`director/playlist_engine.py:89`).
- **Dependencies**: `PlaylistEngine` depends on `SessionLocal` from `core.database` and `guardian_instance` for logging.

## 5. Architecture Documentation
- The system follows a **Service-Engine-Rules** pattern.
- **Anti-repetition** is managed via a JSON history file (`logs/engine_history.json`).
- **Energy Levels** are ranked 1 (Calm) to 5 (Energetic).
